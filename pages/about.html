About HopeFund

We believe every person deserves access to clean water, education, healthcare, and a healthy planet. We're building the world's most trusted crowdfunding platform for social causes.

Our Mission

A World Where Compassion Meets Action

HopeFund was born from a simple belief: the world has enough resources to solve its greatest challenges — it just needs better connections between those who have and those who need.

We bridge the gap between passionate donors and verified social causes, ensuring every rupee creates measurable, transparent impact. No bureaucracy. No waste. Just direct change.

  • ✓ Real-time impact tracking and reporting
  • ✓ Only 5% platform fee — lowest in the industry
  • ✓ Multi-currency donations accepted globally

  • ✓ Full financial transparency reports

The Spark

Co-founders Priya Sharma and Marcus Adeyemi met at a UN climate summit and discovered their shared frustration: powerful causes failing due to lack of funding discovery. HopeFund was founded that same week.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How are campaigns verified? ▼
All campaigns go through a multi-step verification: identity check, organisation registration documents, cause validation, and reference checks. Our team contacts the submitter directly and typically completes verification within 48 hours.
What fees does HopeFund charge? ▼
HopeFund charges a 5% platform fee — the lowest in the industry. Payment processing fees (typically 2.9% + ₹25) are passed through at cost. We're transparent about every deduction and show donors exactly what reaches the cause.
How quickly do organisations receive funds? ▼
Verified organisations receive funds within 3–5 business days for domestic transfers, or 5–10 business days for international transfers. For urgent disaster relief campaigns, we offer expedited 24-hour disbursement.
Can I get a tax deduction for my donation? ▼
Donations to registered non-profit organisations on our platform are typically tax-deductible in the donor's home country. We provide donation receipts automatically, and each campaign page indicates the charity registration status of the recipient.
What happens if a campaign doesn't reach its goal? ▼
HopeFund uses a flexible funding model by default — organisations receive whatever has been raised (excellent for urgent causes). Campaign creators can optionally choose an all-or-nothing model where donors are refunded if the goal isn’t met.
How do I track the impact of my donation? ▼
Every donor receives a personal impact dashboard. Campaign creators are required to post milestone updates at 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% of their goal, plus final impact reports with photos, videos, and verified results.
